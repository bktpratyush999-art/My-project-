# Fighting Game Documentation

## Overview
This fighting game is a fast-paced, action-packed experience that pits players against each other in a battle of skill and strategy. The objective is to defeat your opponent by depleting their health bar, using a variety of attacks, combos, and special moves.

## Game Features
- **Multiple Characters**: Choose from a diverse roster of fighters, each with unique abilities and fighting styles.
- **Combo System**: Execute complex combos to deal extra damage and gain an advantage over your opponent.
- **Special Moves**: Unleash powerful special moves that require timing and precision to perform.
- **Arena Options**: Fight in various arenas, each with its own unique layout and hazards.

## Controls
| Action                | PC           | Console       |
|-----------------------|--------------|---------------|
| Move Left             | A            | Left Stick    |
| Move Right            | D            | Right Stick   |
| Jump                  | Spacebar     | X Button      |
| Attack                | J            | Square Button  |
| Block                 | K            | Circle Button  |
| Special Move          | L            | Triangle Button|

## Game Modes
- **Single Player**: Fight through a series of AI opponents to become the champion.
- **Multiplayer**: Challenge your friends in local or online matches.
- **Tournament Mode**: Compete in a bracket-style tournament to prove your skills.

## Strategies
- Learn the strengths and weaknesses of each character to develop effective strategies.
- Master combos and special moves to maximize your damage output.
- Use the environment to your advantage; some arenas offer unique features that can turn the tide of battle.

## Installation

2. Extract the downloaded files.
3. Run the installer and follow the on-screen instructions.
